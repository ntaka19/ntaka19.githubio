## 1. GDP
* 一定期間において、国内で生産されるすべての最終的な財・サービスの市場価値

### 1.1 Expenditure approach

* **生産**に使用した**額** (最終生産物).

$$
\begin{equation}
GDP = C+I+G+(X-M)
\end{equation}
$$

* C: consumption spending
* I: business investment
* G: Government purchases (investment + consumption)
* X: exports
* M: imports

外需と内需<br>
- C+I+G: 内需
- X-M: 外需 (2字目で覚えるe**x**ports, i**m**ports)




* Y+M: 国内で生産された財・サービスと海外から輸入された財・サービスの合計
* C+I+G+X: 国内での支出の対象＋国外での支出の対象(輸出されることによる) 

$$
\begin{equation}
Y=C+I+G+X-M \Leftrightarrow Y+M=C+I+G+X
\end{equation}
$$



### 1.2 Income approach

## 8. Economic growth

**LOS. 14.m. Sources, measurement, sustainability of economic growth.** <br>

Five sources of economic growth:
1. Labor supply 
2. human capital
3. physical capital stock
4. technology
5. natural resources

Measurement:
* Potential GDP = aggregate hours worked $$\times$$ labor productivity 
* (Sustainable) Growth in potential GDP = growth in labor force + growth in labor production

Sustainable growth:
* the rate of increase in the economy's productive capacity (Potential GDP)


**LOS 14.n Production function approach to analyzing sources of econ-growth** <br>


$$\begin{equation}
Y = A \times f(L,K)
\end{equation}$$

* Y: Aggregate econ output
* L: size of labor force
* K: amount of total capital available
* A: **total factor productivity**
  * amount of output growth that cannot be explained by neither labor nor capital. 


Per-worker basis formulation:

$$\begin{equation}
Y/L = A \times f(K/L)
\end{equation}$$

* diminishing marginal productivity:
* capital deepening investment: increasing physical capita per worker. This does not lead to **long term** sustainable econ growth. 
  * productivity gains and growth of labor force are necessary.
