## GDP


### Expenditure approach

$$
\begin{equation}
GDP = C+I+G+(X-M)
\end{equation}
$$

* C: consumption spending
* I: business investment
* G: Government purchases (investment + consumption)
* X: exports
* M: imports

外需と内需<br>
- C+I+G: 内需
- X-M: 外需

### Income approach


$$
